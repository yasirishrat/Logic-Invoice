Logic Invoice Dashboard


The Dashboard is the first page you will see after you login, and provides you with a quick overview of your Logic Invoice activities. On the left hand side, you will find the navigation bar. You may expand the navigation bar by clicking on the logo on the top left hand corner of the page.
